Breaking 3 Running Club

Breaking 3 Running Club is a specialized, performance-driven community based in Sydney, dedicated to runners chasing the most iconic milestones in amateur marathon running. Whether you are hunting a sub-3-hour finish for men or a sub-3:15 mark for women, B3RC provides the structure, grit, and camaraderie needed to turn those ambitions into reality.

The Pursuit of Excellence

We believe that breaking these barriers requires more than just high mileage; it requires a strategic approach to training, recovery, and mindset. While our primary focus is helping athletes cross those specific time thresholds, our doors are open to everyone. If you have the drive to improve, a passion for the long game, and the heart to support your fellow runners, you belong with B3RC.

Why Run With B3RC?

Targeted Performance

Our collective energy is focused on the sub-3 and sub-3:15 benchmarks, creating a high-level training environment where every session counts.

Sydney-Based Community

From the hills of the Eastern Suburbs to the flat stretches of Olympic Park, we utilize the best of Sydney's terrain to build marathon-ready legs.

Inclusive but Ambitious

"Everyone is welcome" isn't just a slogan. We welcome runners of all backgrounds who are ready to commit to their personal bests.

The Power of the Pack

Just like race-day pacers, we hunt in packs. You'll never have to face a grueling 30km Sunday long run or a localized interval session alone.

Join the Journey

The road to a sub-3 or sub-3:15 marathon is long, often "a bit crazy," and incredibly rewarding. Don't chase the clock in isolation. Come be part of a vibrant, supportive, and disciplined community that understands exactly what it takes to reach the finish line ahead of the pack.

B3RC: Your journey to the sub-3 mark starts here.
